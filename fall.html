FALL

AT HOLLOW CREEK FARM

Fall brings crisp air, pumpkin picking, and colorful harvest traditions across the farm. Families can enjoy weekend hayrides, sip fresh apple cider, and celebrate at the Fall Harvest Festival.

Seasonal Experiences

Pumpkin Patch

More than twenty pumpkin varieties available for U-Pick and pre-picked purchases.

Weekend Hayrides

Hayrides run every weekend through October with scenic rides around the farm.

Fresh Apple Cider

Fresh apple cider pressed on-site daily and sold by the jug throughout fall.

Harvest Festival

Food vendors, live music, and craft fairs during the final October weekends.
